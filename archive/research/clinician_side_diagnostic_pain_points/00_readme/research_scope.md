# Research scope

## What was researched

Clinician-side pain points about identifying health issues early, especially hidden, delayed, missed, complex, or long-term conditions.

## Included

- Medical literature
- Diagnostic-error literature
- Patient-safety reports
- Public health-system frameworks
- Clinician commentary where available
- EHR usability and documentation studies
- Triage, handoff, referral, and low-resource/community-health evidence

## Excluded

- New research beyond the completed Research D pack
- Private patient data
- Unsourced interpretation
- Full copyrighted source copies

## Boundaries

This package organizes the prior completed research. It does not verify every quotation against the live web again, and some metadata remains `unknown` where the prior research did not capture it.

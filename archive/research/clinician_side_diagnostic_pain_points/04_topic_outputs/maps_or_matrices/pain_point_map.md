# Pain-point map

| Pain point | Stage | Roles | Main implication |
|---|---|---|---|
| Time pressure | first assessment/history | PCPs/GPs | Previsit timeline and concise context |
| Alert overload | result follow-up | PCPs/covering clinicians | Risk-ranked result worklist |
| Fragmented care | repeat visits/referral | PCPs/specialists | Diagnostic episode tracking |
| Triage overload | arrival/nurse triage | nurses/ED staff | Red flags and reassessment timers |
| Handoff failure | shift change/inpatient | nurses/doctors | Structured handoff with pending tasks |
| Fatigue | all stages | all staff | Workload-aware safety checks |
| Low-resource limits | screening/testing/referral | CHWs/clinicians | Resource-aware support |

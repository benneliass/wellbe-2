# What clinicians need earlier

- Concise longitudinal patient timeline
- Risk-ranked abnormal-result worklist
- Clear owner for every pending test, referral, and unresolved symptom
- Pre-visit patient narrative plus structured red flags
- View of what is still unexplained
- Repeat-visit and deterioration flags
- Prior labs/imaging trends and pending action states
- Referral status visible to primary and specialty care
- Triage support under load
- Guardrails against diagnostic overshadowing
- Resource-aware options for low-resource settings
- Feedback loops on missed, delayed, or revised diagnoses

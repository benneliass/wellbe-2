# High-confidence findings

- Diagnostic safety is a system problem across settings, supported by AHRQ, CDC, WHO, and National Academies sources.
- EHR alert overload and result-follow-up failures are real clinician-side safety risks.
- Continuity failures and repeated unresolved visits can delay diagnosis.
- Staff fatigue contributes to patient safety risk and is under-captured in formal safety systems.
- Diagnostic access gaps are severe in low-resource settings.

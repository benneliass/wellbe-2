# Limitations

- Some metadata remains incomplete, especially author/organization and exact publication dates.
- Raw source files were not embedded; placeholder files preserve URLs and context.
- Quantitative metrics should be verified against the original linked sources before publication or statistical use.
- Evidence quality varies by source type; patient stories and complaints are valuable for workflow insight but are not population estimates.
- The workbook prioritized breadth across settings and regions, not exhaustive coverage of any one country or condition.

# Research scope

## Included
- Real health-system workflows for identifying health issues from entry/contact through triage, assessment, testing, referral, discharge, and follow-up.
- Care settings: emergency departments, urgent care, primary care, community health centers, rural/low-resource clinics, telehealth, specialist pathways, hospital outpatient diagnostics, preventive screening, pharmacy/community/home visits.
- Regions represented in the completed workbook: US, UK/England/Scotland/Wales, Israel/Middle East, Europe, India, East Asia, Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa, and global rural/low-resource settings.
- Successful and failed identification flows, including black zones where important signals can be lost.

## Excluded or limited
- No new research was conducted during packaging.
- Raw PDFs/web pages were not newly downloaded; source placeholder notes were created instead.
- Informal clinic reviews and public forums were not deeply represented in the original workbook.
- Private or identifying patient data was not added.

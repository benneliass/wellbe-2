# Contradictions and differing realities

- Guidelines describe clear ownership and escalation routes, while patient-safety reports document failures in exactly those transitions.
- Digital triage can improve access and routing, but it can also narrow the story into a single symptom or exclude patients with poor digital access.
- Screening programs identify hidden disease risk at population scale, yet abnormal-screen follow-up may fail because of practical barriers.
- Specialist referral can improve diagnostic accuracy, but wrong-specialty routing or rejected referrals can extend delay.

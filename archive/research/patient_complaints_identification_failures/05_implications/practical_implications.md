# Practical implications

- Ask patients for their top concern in their own words.
- Record what remains unexplained, not only what was ruled out.
- Treat repeated visits for the same/worsening symptom as a safety signal.
- Ensure every abnormal result has an owner and patient notification status.
- Make referral outcomes clear to both patient and referring clinician.
- Use family/caregiver concern as a clinical signal, especially for deterioration.
- Provide clear safety-net instructions at discharge.

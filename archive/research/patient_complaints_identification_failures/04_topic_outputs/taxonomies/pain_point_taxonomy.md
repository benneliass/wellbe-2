# Pain-point taxonomy

- Dismissed / silenced: patient narrative not captured.
- One-symptom tunnel vision: multiple concerns reduced to one issue.
- Normal-test trap: normal tests used to close unresolved symptoms.
- Abnormal result black hole: result exists but has no accountable follow-up.
- Repeated visits ignored: pattern across time not recognized.
- Age/gender minimization: serious symptoms normalized by demographic assumptions.
- Wrong-specialty loop: referral destination does not address original red flag.
- Documentation ambiguity: patient-facing record unclear or contradictory.
- Discharge without ownership: unresolved symptoms or tasks lack responsible person.
- Positive listening: time, explanation, physical exam, whole-person context, and continuity.

# Contradictions and different realities

- Some patient stories describe doctors as dismissive; positive reviews show that similar settings can produce careful listening and whole-person care.
- Normal tests can be reassuring in many cases, but several cases show normal tests becoming unsafe when symptoms remain severe or discordant.
- Referral refusal may mean no pathway, insufficient information, or clinical disagreement; patients may experience all of these as dismissal.
- Advocacy stories often emphasize patient voice and long delay; official reports often emphasize process and accountability but include fewer direct quotes.

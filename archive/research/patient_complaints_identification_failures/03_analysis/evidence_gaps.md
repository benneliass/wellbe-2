# Evidence gaps

- Limited raw screenshots/exports of review pages were packaged.
- Some source publication dates were not available from the completed research.
- No complete search strategy, search terms, or deduplication log was preserved.
- Public-review evidence is not clinically adjudicated.
- Underrepresented regions include Latin America, Middle East, East Asia, and rural/tribal/community-health systems beyond India/Nigeria examples.
- Pharmacy-based care and telehealth patient-review evidence were lightly covered compared with ED/primary care/specialist settings.

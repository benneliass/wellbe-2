# Patient journey failure points

- Before visit: symptoms undocumented or normalized.
- Arrival/registration/intake: complaint shortened or miscoded.
- Triage: urgency missed or repeat visit context absent.
- First assessment/history/exam: incomplete history, bias, or minimization.
- Labs/imaging: abnormal result or trend missed; normal-test trap.
- Referral: wrong specialty, delay, no completion tracking.
- Follow-up/repeat visit: no owner connects the dots.
- Specialist review: fragmented records obscure longitudinal pattern.

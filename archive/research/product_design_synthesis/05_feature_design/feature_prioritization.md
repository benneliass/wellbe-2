# Feature prioritization

## High value / lower risk

- Patient narrative capture
- Concise timeline summary
- Missing data checklist
- Referral and follow-up status tracking

## High value / high risk

- Red-flag highlighting
- Lab trend detection
- Repeat-visit escalation

## Needs evidence

- Any feature without explicit supporting source or evidence IDs.

## Region-specific

- Low-resource offline/community workflows
- EHR-integrated high-resource workflows

## Unsafe or caution-needed

- Features that imply diagnosis, overrule clinicians, create panic language, or drive over-testing.

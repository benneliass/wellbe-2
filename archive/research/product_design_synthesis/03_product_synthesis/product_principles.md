# Product principles

1. Support clinician judgment; do not replace it.
2. Make patient narratives easier to capture, preserve, and review.
3. Track unresolved symptoms, repeat visits, abnormal results, and referrals over time.
4. Keep summaries concise and explain why a signal is highlighted.
5. Reduce documentation burden and alert fatigue.
6. Preserve uncertainty and avoid final diagnostic claims.
7. Build for regional and resource differences.
8. Require safety governance for high-risk flags, bias risks, false positives, and false negatives.

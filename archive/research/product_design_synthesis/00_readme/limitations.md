# Limitations

- Raw webpages, PDFs, and screenshots were generally not included.
- Some metadata is incomplete.
- Public complaint/review evidence is not always clinically adjudicated.
- Non-English and regional coverage remains uneven.
- Product insights are evidence-backed synthesis outputs, not validated medical claims.
- Some traceability fields are marked `unknown` when IDs were not present in completed outputs.
- No attached evidence package was found to be corrupted or unreadable.

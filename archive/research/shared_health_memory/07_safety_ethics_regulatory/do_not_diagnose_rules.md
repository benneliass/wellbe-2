# Do-Not-Diagnose Rules

1. Do not present the system as a final diagnostic authority.
2. Use language like "unresolved," "needs review," "pending," or "source-linked pattern," not definitive diagnostic claims.
3. Require clinician review for escalation, red-flag, medication, care-routing, or high-risk suggestions.
4. Show sources and uncertainty for every summary or flag.
5. Distinguish patient-reported, clinician-documented, and system-inferred content.
6. Do not hide uncertainty to make the product feel smarter.
7. Do not encourage over-testing without clinical review.

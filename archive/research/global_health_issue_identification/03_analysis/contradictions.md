# Contradictions and different realities

- Gatekeeping can improve continuity, but it can also create referral delays if queues and IT workflows fail.
- Open-access specialist systems can improve choice and speed, but can weaken diagnostic ownership.
- Integrated digital records can reduce fragmentation, but underserved communities may still fall outside usable workflows.
- Private care can speed access for people who can pay, but can worsen system-level fragmentation and missing records.
- Telehealth can reduce distance barriers, but only when broadband, language access, and follow-up workflows exist.

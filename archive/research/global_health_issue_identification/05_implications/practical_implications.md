# Practical implications

- Ask what happened before the current visit, including informal, private, pharmacy, traditional, and cross-border care.
- Treat repeated visits for the same symptom as a safety signal.
- Track test/referral completion, not just orders placed.
- Pair normal test results with symptom status.
- Use professional interpreters and translated written instructions where needed.
- Include family/community concern where culturally appropriate and desired by the patient.
- Make referral completion visible in low-resource and rural settings.

# Research scope

## Researched

Global, cultural, and resource-level differences in real-world workflows for identifying health issues, especially long-term, hidden, missed, delayed, or complex conditions.

Covered regions and system types include the US, UK, Israel, Europe, India, East Asia, Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa, rural high-income settings, rural low-income settings, Indigenous/underserved communities, migrant/refugee settings, public/private systems, insurance-based and universal systems, GP-gatekeeper systems, hospital-first/open-access systems, and community-health-worker systems.

## Included

Medical literature, health-system profiles, patient-safety reports, ombudsman complaints, public health-system reports, documented case studies, and implementation examples.

## Excluded

This packaging step did not add new research. It did not systematically download full raw webpages or PDFs. It did not include private patient data.

# Analysis

Cross-research synthesis outputs for the WellBe project.

## Files

### `wellbe-research-synthesis.canvas.tsx`

A Cursor Canvas (interactive React artifact) that synthesizes all 6 research packages against the WellBe product vision document (`/Users/benel/personal-repos/wellbe/docs/wellbe-overview.md`).

**What it covers:**
- Vision alignment map — every major WellBe module and principle rated against research evidence (Confirmed / Needs Extension / Challenged / Critical Gap / Opportunity)
- Three cross-cutting insights that emerged independently across multiple research streams
- Research F evidence-graded feature backlog — 26 living insights (LI-001–LI-026) with status, risk level, and guardrail for each
- 14-stage black zone map — every patient journey failure point with prevention mechanism
- Research F product principles and do-not-diagnose rules
- Strategic recommendations: what to confirm, what to add urgently, and the two vision shifts worth arguing about

**Research sources:**
| Package | Scope | Sources |
|---|---|---|
| Research A — Health System Patient Flows | Real-world workflows across care settings | 56 |
| Research B — Missed Signals & Diagnostic Errors | Documented cases of missed/delayed diagnosis | 15 |
| Research C — Patient Complaints & Identification Failures | Patient voice, ombudsman reports, safety cases | 39 |
| Research D — Clinician-Side Diagnostic Pain Points | Clinician views, EHR burden, handoff failures | 24 |
| Research E — Global Health Issue Identification | Cross-system, cross-culture, low/high-resource | 30 |
| Research F — Product Design Synthesis | Evidence-graded features, black zones, schema, safety | 164+ consolidated |

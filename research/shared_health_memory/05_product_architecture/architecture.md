# Product Architecture

## Layer 1: Capture

Inputs include patient story, caregiver story, intake, prior visits, labs, imaging, notes, referrals, medications, patient-generated data, and uploaded documents.

## Layer 2: Health Thread Builder

Connects related signals into unresolved or ongoing Health Threads.

## Layer 3: Evidence Graph

Every statement should link back to its source: patient-reported, clinician-documented, lab, imaging, referral, or note.

## Layer 4: Safety and Responsibility Engine

Tracks pending tests, abnormal results, referrals, repeat visits, unresolved symptoms, correction requests, and ownership gaps.

## Layer 5: Clinician View

Shows concise source-linked summaries, timeline, unresolved questions, missing data, pending items, and patient words.

## Layer 6: Patient View

Shows what is understood, what is unresolved, what is pending, who owns the next step, when to follow up, and how to correct missing/wrong information.

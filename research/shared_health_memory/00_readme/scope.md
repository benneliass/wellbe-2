# Scope

## Included

- Product framing for a medical second-brain approach.
- Research-backed facts from health records, OpenNotes/OurNotes/OurDX, diagnostic safety, patient-generated health data, follow-up literature, interoperability standards, and safety/regulatory guidance.
- A proposed product framework: Shared Health Memory.
- A proposed key object: Health Thread.
- MVP recommendations and high-risk feature warnings.

## Excluded

- New primary clinical research.
- New diagnosis claims.
- Treatment recommendations.
- Private patient data.
- External source expansion beyond the sources used for the prior synthesis answer.

## Intended use

Use this as a product strategy package for shaping a health platform focused on unresolved, missed, delayed, or complex health issues.

## Not intended for

- Clinical decision-making without clinician review.
- Regulatory approval evidence.
- Medical advice to patients.

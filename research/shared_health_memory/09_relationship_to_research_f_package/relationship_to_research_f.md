# Relationship to Research F Product-Design Synthesis

This package builds on the prior Research F synthesis direction but is focused specifically on the "second brain" idea.

## How it connects

Research F identified product needs around:

- richer intake
- longitudinal tracking
- doctor-facing summaries
- black-zone prevention
- patient voice protection
- clinician workflow support
- safety and ethics

The Shared Health Memory approach reframes those needs into a single product model:

- Health Threads for unresolved issues
- six memory types
- source-linked summaries
- follow-up closure
- patient correction
- clinician review

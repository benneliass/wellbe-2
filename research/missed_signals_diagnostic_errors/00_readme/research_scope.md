# Research scope

## What was researched

Documented real-world cases and patterns where health issues were missed, delayed, dismissed, or discovered late, especially long-term, hidden, complex, or hard-to-identify conditions.

## Included

- Medical case analyses and patient-safety reports.
- Diagnostic-error and delayed-diagnosis sources.
- Patient advocacy stories and public patient voice where already public.
- Multiple conditions and settings including emergency, primary care, specialist care, public health screening, low-resource clinics, and chronic illness contexts.

## Excluded

- New research after the completed workbook.
- Private patient data not already public.
- Full raw copyrighted articles.
- Unsourced interpretation.

## Boundaries

The case library is curated for workflow pattern discovery. It is not a prevalence estimate and should not be read as statistically representative.

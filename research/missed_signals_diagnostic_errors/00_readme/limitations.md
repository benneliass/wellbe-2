# Limitations

- Some metadata fields such as author, exact publication date, or outcome are unknown in the completed workbook and are marked `unknown`.
- Raw source documents were not preserved except for the completed workbook and available preview image.
- Patient quotes are intentionally short and contextual; this package does not include full patient stories.
- Counts are based on curated case-library tags, not epidemiologic prevalence.
- Some evidence comes from patient advocacy or experience sources and should be treated as lower-confidence for clinical generalization while still valuable for workflow discovery.

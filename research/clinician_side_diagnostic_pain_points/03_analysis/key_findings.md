# Key findings

1. Diagnosis often fails across handoffs and time, not only inside one clinician's reasoning.
2. EHR alert overload and result follow-up are major black zones.
3. Repeated visits and unresolved symptoms need explicit tracking.
4. Online intake and structured triage can lose complexity and prior history.
5. Referral loops often lack clear ownership.
6. Clinician fatigue, time pressure, and cognitive overload reduce diagnostic bandwidth.
7. Triage and handover are diagnostic-safety stages, not only administrative steps.
8. Low-resource settings require workflows that reflect limited test/referral access.

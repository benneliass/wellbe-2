# Patterns

- **No single owner:** Tests, referrals, and unresolved symptoms can fall between PCPs, specialists, patients, and admin systems.
- **Signal hidden in volume:** Alerts, notes, inboxes, and copied-forward documentation can bury abnormal results and changing symptoms.
- **Longitudinal context missing:** Clinicians often need history, trends, and prior visits earlier than the current workflow gives them.
- **Front-door triage risk:** Intake forms, nurse triage, and online consultation tools shape what reaches the clinician.
- **Human limits matter:** Fatigue, time pressure, emotional strain, and difficult interactions affect diagnostic work.
- **Resource constraints shape diagnosis:** In low-resource settings, the problem may be absence of tests, transport, or referral capacity rather than interpretation alone.

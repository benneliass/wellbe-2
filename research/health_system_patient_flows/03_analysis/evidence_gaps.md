# Evidence gaps

- More direct clinic-review and forum data would help capture everyday patient-experience failures.
- More non-English sources are needed for East Asia, Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa, and Middle East workflows.
- More pharmacy-based and occupational-health workflows would broaden front-door coverage.
- More exact timing metrics are needed for referral delays, abnormal-result follow-up, and repeat-visit escalation.

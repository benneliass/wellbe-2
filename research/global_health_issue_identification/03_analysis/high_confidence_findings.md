# High-confidence findings

- Diagnostic error and delay are major safety problems even in high-resource systems.
- Repeat presentations and lack of continuity are major missed-diagnosis risks.
- Abnormal-result follow-up needs closed-loop ownership.
- Referral completion is different from referral placement.
- Rural access barriers include provider scarcity, travel, transportation, and broadband limitations.
- Language access and cultural safety affect diagnostic identification, not only patient experience.

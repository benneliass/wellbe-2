# Repeated patterns

- Repeat visits not recognized as one unresolved problem.
- Abnormal imaging/lab findings not closed with a clear owner.
- Normal tests interpreted as closure despite persistent symptoms.
- Referrals placed but not completed.
- Private/informal/out-of-system care not visible to the formal record.
- Language, culture, and mistrust blocking history-taking and follow-up.
- Rural and low-resource diagnostic capacity concentrated far from first contact.
- CHW workflows working well when protocols, supplies, and referral loops exist.

# Extracted evidence notes

The extracted evidence separates:

- `facts.csv`: factual claims from sources.
- `statistics_or_metrics.csv`: numeric findings.
- `quotes.csv`: short direct quote snippets with context.
- `cases_or_examples.csv`: documented scenarios or case examples.
- `evidence_items.csv`: master evidence table combining facts and cases.

Rows use `unknown` where metadata was not available from the completed research notes.

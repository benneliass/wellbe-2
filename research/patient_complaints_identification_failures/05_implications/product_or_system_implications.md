# Product or system implications

- Persistent symptom timeline across visits and providers.
- Concern-by-concern visit closure with visible disposition.
- Red-flag prompts that override age/gender normalization.
- Normal-test safety net that keeps unexplained severe symptoms open.
- Abnormal-result ownership ledger with due dates and escalation.
- Referral transparency layer distinguishing no pathway, rejection, wrong specialty, or more information needed.
- Patient/family escalation route for deterioration concerns.
- Diagnosis-status clarity in patient portals.
- Low-resource diagnostic provenance: lab source, equipment quality, affordability, and fallback referral options.

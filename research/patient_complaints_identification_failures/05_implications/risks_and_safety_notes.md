# Risks and safety notes

- Public complaints should not be used to accuse individual clinicians without review of full records.
- Patient language should be preserved respectfully and not overinterpreted.
- Diagnostic-support products can cause alert fatigue if every symptom becomes a red flag.
- Patient-facing tools must avoid creating false certainty or encouraging self-diagnosis without care pathways.
- Low-resource settings require caution: the problem may be infrastructure, cost, or access, not only clinician reasoning.

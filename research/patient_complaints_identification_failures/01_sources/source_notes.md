# Source notes

The source set intentionally mixes evidence types:

- High-confidence sources: official ombudsman, HDC, HSSIB, AHRQ/PSNet, NCBI/AHRQ review, and peer-reviewed/academic sources.
- Medium-confidence sources: detailed public patient reviews and advocacy group patient stories.
- Low-confidence sources: news reports and public patient accounts with limited clinical verification.

Public patient voice is used to understand language, pain points, and workflow signals. It should not be treated as proof of clinical negligence without checking the original records or official findings.

# Extracted evidence notes

The extracted evidence is split by purpose:

- `evidence_items.csv`: broad master table focused on cases/examples.
- `facts.csv`: factual claims from stronger sources or source-level summaries.
- `quotes.csv`: short direct quotes with context and source.
- `cases_or_examples.csv`: documented stories, incidents, and scenarios.
- `reviews_or_complaints.csv`: public reviews, complaints, and patient-experience reports.
- `statistics_or_metrics.csv`: delays and quantitative details captured from cases.

Unknown fields are intentionally left as `unknown` rather than inferred.

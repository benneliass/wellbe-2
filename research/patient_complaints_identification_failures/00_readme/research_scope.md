# Research scope

## What was researched

Public patient complaints, reviews, advocacy stories, official complaint reports, patient-safety reports, and selected literature related to health-system identification failures: missed, delayed, hidden, complex, long-term, or poorly followed-up conditions.

## Included

- Public patient reviews and complaints.
- Patient and family quotes.
- Advocacy group patient stories.
- Ombudsman and commissioner decisions.
- Patient-safety case reviews.
- Examples from UK, New Zealand, Nigeria, India, and the United States/international safety literature.
- Positive examples where listening, continuity, or follow-up worked.

## Excluded

- New research after the completed Research C pass.
- Private or non-public patient data.
- Full copyrighted source copies.
- Generic complaints without diagnostic-identification context.

## Boundaries

This archive is qualitative and exploratory. It is not a prevalence study and should not be used to estimate rates of diagnostic error.

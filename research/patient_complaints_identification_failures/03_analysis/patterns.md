# Repeated patterns

- Doctor did not listen / patient narrative cut off.
- Symptom dismissed as normal, age-related, anxiety/stress, or too unlikely.
- Normal-test trap: a normal or inconclusive test closed the encounter without explaining symptoms.
- Abnormal result not followed up or not communicated to patient.
- Repeated visits ignored; no one connected the dots across time.
- Wrong specialty or unclear referral pathway.
- Documentation or portal confusion about diagnosis status.
- Discharge without unresolved-problem plan.
- Patient/family escalation required to get attention.
- Positive counter-pattern: listened, examined, explained, safety-netted, and followed up.

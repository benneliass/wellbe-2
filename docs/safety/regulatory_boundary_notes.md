# Regulatory boundary notes

This package does not determine regulatory classification. It highlights boundary risk.

## Lower-risk framing

- personal memory
- source-linked summaries
- education
- visit preparation
- patient-authored timelines
- pending item reminders based on user-entered/imported facts

## Higher-risk framing

- diagnosing disease
- ruling conditions in/out
- triaging urgency autonomously
- ranking medical risk without transparent basis
- recommending treatment changes
- clinician worklists that direct clinical action

## Required before high-risk release

- jurisdiction-specific regulatory review
- clinical safety case
- hazard analysis
- validation against representative scenarios
- false-positive/false-negative monitoring
- human review requirements
